The Language of My Soul

Across the miles, where silence may grow,
Your voice’s warmth still softly flows.
Though distance tests and nights run long,
Our hearts beat steady, brave, and strong.

Oh, those blue eyes that remind me of the ocean
Could be the sky too, so vast and divine.
I’d be lost, adrift in that endless hue,
A willing captive, forever with you.

Your giggles and smiles, they melt my heart,
Each moment with you feels like a dream’s part.
Your nose, your lips I’ve yet to kiss,
A longing I cradle in dawn’s soft mist.

No words can measure how my love grew;
All I know is I’d love and cherish you.
With gentle head scratches to soothe and calm,
And whispers that vow you’re safe in my arms.

May our love endure, may it thrive,
Through barren days and sparkless nights.
I’ll hold your hand, both fierce and near,
A promise to stay through doubt and fear.

Je t’aime, Saranghae, Mahal kita, Te quiero,
None can capture the depth of my love for you.
So let my soul be the language you hear,
Forever yours, in every word, I swear.
